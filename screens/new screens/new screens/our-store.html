HOW TO FIND US

BY CAR

From Lekki Toll Gate, head towards Victoria Island. Take the third exit at the roundabout onto Admiralty Way. Continue straight for 2km, then turn right onto Victoria Island Boulevard. Our store will be on your left.

BY PUBLIC TRANSPORT

Take the BRT bus to Lekki Phase 1 terminal. From there, board a taxi or ride-hailing service to Victoria Island Boulevard. The store is a 10-minute ride from the terminal.

FROM AIRPORT

Murtala Muhammed International Airport is approximately 45 minutes away by car. We recommend using our preferred taxi service for a comfortable journey.
